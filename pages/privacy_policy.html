Privacy Policy

Applicability
This privacy notice applies only to the Rain Garden App and explains the University of Connecticut Center for Land Use Education and Research (CLEAR) practices concerning the collection, use, and disclosure of user information. User information collected by the Rain Garden App will only be used as outlined in the Privacy Notice.

Collection and Use
Except as provided below, we do not collect or use the technical information discussed in this section to identify individual users.

Passive/Automatic Collection
The Rain Garden App does not collect any technical or personal information unless authorized or offered by the user as described below.

Active/Manual/Voluntary Collection
The mapping features of the Rain Garden App do not use location information unless specifically authorized by the user. Location information is only retained in the app if the user voluntarily chooses to save it. Information saved to the app in the My Rain Gardens section is not accessible to or collected by UConn CLEAR.
A user may choose to voluntarily provide information to UConn CLEAR via the "Share" and "Feedback" features of the Rain Garden App. Any information voluntarily provided to UConn CLEAR is retained in accordance with the State of Connecticut Records Retention Schedule Requirements. The voluntary provision of this information indicates the user's consent to the collection, use, storage, and disclosure of this information.

This app uses the Google Analytics (http://www.google.com/analytics) SDK to track general usage statistics and performance. No personally identifiable data is collected through Google Analytics (except for information voluntarily provided via the "Feedback" or "Share" features as noted above). Data collected is used to track the number of active users, usage of app screens and features, and the number and type of application crashes.

Information Usage
Information provided to UConn CLEAR voluntarily by app users may be used to identify rain garden locations and potentially be placed on a website indicating those locations. Any and all requests for limits on this use by owners will be honored.

Use of Third Party Services
The Rain Garden App uses YouTube for the videos embedded in the App. Users should refer to YouTube's privacy page (http://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy) for more information.

The Rain Garden App connects to the mapping features through ESRI's ArcGIS Online. Users should refer to ESRI's privacy policy (http://www.esri.com/legal/privacy) for more information

Privacy Notice Changes
UConn CLEAR may make changes to this policy in the future. Any such changes will be consistent with our commitment to user privacy and will be clearly posted in a revised privacy notice. Only data collected from the time of the revision forward will be used under the new policy.

Contact Us
If you feel that this app is not following its stated policy and communicating with UConn CLEAR does not resolve the matter, or if you have general questions or concerns about privacy or data security at the University of Connecticut, please contact the University's Privacy officer at (860) 486-5256 or rachel.krinsky@uconn.edu, or the Information Security Office at security@uconn.edu or (860) 486-8255.
